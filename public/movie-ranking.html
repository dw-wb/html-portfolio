Great Movies That You Probably Haven't Watch

My 3 most recent discoveries!

Dersu Uzala (1975)

The Russian army sends an explorer on an expedition to the snowy Siberian wilderness where he makes friends with a seasoned local hunter. Beautiful movie.

I Served the King of England (2006)

A short and clever man tells the story of his life working as a waiter in Luxury Hotels in Pre-World War II Prague. Really entertaining!

Together (2000)

In 1975, the dynamics of a Swedish commune begin to change upon the arrival of a beaten wife and her two kids.
